Changes in the number of jobs before and after Covid-19

It can be seen that due to the COVID-19, the number of jobs in health care and social assistance increased by 16K, while the demand for jobs in education and training decreased by 15K.

The demand for jobs related to professional, scientific and technical services are unaffected by COVID-19.
